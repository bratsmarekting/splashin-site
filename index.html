about

SPLASHIN SATURDAYS & SUNDAYS

Where daytime energy, poolside music, summer fashion, DJs, drinks, and a beautiful social crowd come together for the kind of afternoon people actually want.

It’s one of Calgary’s standout summer day-party experiences, built around sunshine, music, friends, photos, drinks, dancing, and poolside energy.

From bachelorette pool parties and girls’ days to birthdays, couples, crews, and music lovers, Splashin Saturdays & Sundays is fun, stylish, social, and easy to show up for.

Think Calgary rooftop pool parties at the Ramada Rooftop Pool and courtyard pool day parties at the Carriage House Courtyard Pool. It’s a true DJ pool party experience in Calgary, with floaties in the water, drinks in hand, and a crowd that makes the afternoon feel alive.

Bring your people, step into the poolside scene, and let the afternoon turn into the kind of summer day Calgary needs more of.

Splashin is where Calgary comes to feel the summer vibe.

FAQ

What time do the parties run?
Saturdays run 12–5 PM at the Ramada Plaza rooftop pool downtown, and Sundays run 12–4 PM at the Carriage House. Your exact entry details are on your ticket.
Where are the parties held?
Two venues: the Carriage House Hotel & Conference Centre (9030 Macleod Trail South) on Sundays, and the Ramada Plaza by Wyndham Calgary Downtown (708 8th Avenue SW) on Saturdays. Tap “Directions” on either venue for a map.
How do I get tickets?
Tickets are sold per date through our ticketing page — just tap “Get Tickets” on any date in the calendar above. Every party is capacity-capped, and early bird tickets are limited.
How much are tickets?
Carriage House (Sundays): $25 early bird / $30 regular. Ramada (Saturdays): $30 regular. July 5 (Stampede Edition): $40 flat. Early bird tickets are limited, so they go first — all prices in CAD.
Is there an age requirement?
18+. Valid government-issued photo ID is required at entry. For family details, reach out through the Connect Us link at the bottom of the page.
What’s included with my ticket?
Entry, pool access, and the full DJ experience. Food and drinks are available for purchase on site. At the Carriage House, seating is first come, first served. At the Ramada, cabanas and reserved seating are available at an additional cost — reach out through Connect Us or email hello@splashinsaturdayssundays.ca.
Can I bring a group, or book a birthday / bachelorette?
Absolutely — groups and celebrations are our specialty. Tap “Book Group” or email groups@splashinsaturdayssundays.ca, and we’ll help you sort out the details.
What should I bring?
Swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, your wallet, and valid photo ID. Towels aren’t included — a limited number are available for purchase on site, but we recommend bringing your own.
What’s your refund & weather policy?
The event happens rain or shine. If a lightning and thunderstorm before the event forces us to cancel the show, refunds will be processed.
Can I book a hotel room at the venue?
Yes — both venues are hotels. Tap “Book Room” on either venue or email hotelrooms@splashinsaturdayssundays.ca, and we’ll help arrange it.
